Editor's Note

Every single vibe coding piece today is a real production workflow. Anthropic's security team, NVIDIA's engineering org, European car marketplaces — these aren't demos, they're shipped systems handling actual work.

Two things stand out. The infrastructure is stabilizing fast. Vercel's natural language firewall rules, Google's durable agent state machines, Windsurf's 2.5x speed boost — the tooling layer is maturing faster than I expected six months ago. And the domain specificity is getting tighter. Finance MBRs, security threat detection, procedural game generation. The general-purpose productivity phase is over.

But the capabilities research tells a different story. Cerebras IPO betting on inference architecture splits, the 'end of finetuning era,' xAI dissolving into SpaceX. That's all structural change happening above the builder layer. We might be in a temporary sweet spot — infrastructure reliable enough to ship on, but the foundational shifts haven't destabilized everything yet.
